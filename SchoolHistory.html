History Of SICS JWALAMUKHI

The school was founded by Shivalik international Convent School Society in 2014 under the inspiration of Smt. Nasib Devi W/O Late Sh. Hari Singh Bajota . Shivalik International Convent School is an integral part one of the finest centre of learing in Himachal Pradesh. It offers its students all the advantages of a model educational system without the distractions of modern city life situated at a distance of 55 kms. From MATA CHINTAPURNI, it is where rural India meet the modern west, giving the student the benefit of what is best in the two.


Geographical Location


Jawalamukhi is connected with Highway Roads Palampur to Mata Jwalamukhi and Hamirpur To Mata Jawala Mukhi . There is regular bus service between Dhaliara to Jawalamukhi & between Mata Chintpurni to Jawalamukhi. It is four hour journey by car from Chandigarh two hours from Hoshiarpur & 3 hour from Pathankot. The climate of Jawalamukhi is excellent and is considered good for health.
